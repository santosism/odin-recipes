Hot Dog Recipe

Description

This is a hot dog, made of some assorted materials, meatlike I'm sure. But it goes well at barbecues, at ball games, movie theaters, on a cheap night at home, or just bored and a little hungry: hot dogs cooked in cooking wine.

Ingredients

  • Hot Dog Buns
  • Nathan's Famous Hot Dogs packet
  • Stove
  • Cooking Wine

Steps

  1. Place hot dog buns on clean plate, on counter
  2. Turn on stove, place pan on stove, medium heat
  3. Open and pour some cooking wine on the pan
  4. Grab Nathan's Famous Hot Dogs packet of four, cut open haphazardly, place two on pan
  5. Pour more cooking wine on the pan
  6. Cook for five minutes, turning the hot dogs over to get an even coat on them
