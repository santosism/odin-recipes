Omelette Recipe

Description

An omelette is usually a nice dish, made up of many cracked egg yolks scrambled together and cooked on a stove. Although sometimes the egg shells show up, or the omelette itself is less a perfect circle than a cracked and burnt thing.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Stove

Steps

  1. Take out three eggs from carton of eggs in refrigerator and place on counter
  2. Turn on stovetop, place pan, medium heat
  3. Grab bowl, preferably clean, and crack the eggs
  4. Using a fork, mix quickly the eggy concoction
  5. With the stove sufficiently hot, pour the eggy mess onto the hot pan
  6. Let cook for five to ten minutes, flip when necessary or bored
  7. Turn off stove, have clean plate ready on the counter. Use spatula to gently pry the omelette off the pan
  8. Carefully, move plate closer to the pan, and using the spatula, flip omelette onto the plate
  9. Eat, and be merry
